## Good First Components

If you are just getting started, begin with `Button`, `Card`, and `Input`. Those three components cover a large share of everyday product UI and make it easy to establish the look and feel of a screen quickly.

## Built For Product Screens

These docs are aimed at the parts of an app people use every day: onboarding flows, profile pages, settings, dashboards, and forms. Start with the defaults, then shape them around your product rather than rebuilding the basics from scratch.
